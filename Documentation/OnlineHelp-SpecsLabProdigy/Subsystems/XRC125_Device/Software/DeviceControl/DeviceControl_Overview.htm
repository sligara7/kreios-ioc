XRC-125 MF Device Control

The XRC-125 MF Device Control allows you to control the operation of the XRC-125 MF and hence the μ-FOCUS 350. In addition to operating the instrument, it features routines to calibrate intensity and resolution.

 

The following topics are covered:

■    Operating the source using presets.

■    Manually setting operating parameters.

■    Changing preset values.

■    Viewing device status.

■    Performing anode overview scans and setting positions.

■    Performing anode scans to check the electron beam width.

■    Checking the intensity of the electron beam and calibrating motors.

 

There is also a note about the log feature.

 

 

Before accessing the XRC-125 MF, you need to establish a connection:

■    Click Control. The title bar of the Device Control will turn blue to indicate the connection. The icon in the bar will turn green to indicate that the device is ready for operation.

 

NOTE:
The device can also be included in experiments. See also the Experiment Editor reference documentation.

 

Clicking the Disconnect button breaks contact with the instrument. All controls are inactive.

 

There are two buttons, and , which collapse and expand the Device Control respectively. These give you access to additional features or allow you to hide unneeded features in the interest of clarity.

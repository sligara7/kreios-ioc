Switching On the Analyzer

After connecting to the analyzer, you can edit the settings and start the analyzer. The initial view of the Device Control contains the settings necessary for basic operation. These are described in the table below.

 

 

Parameter Description
Kinetic Energy Kinetic energy of detected particles. Left column is the target voltage that you set; right column is the current value of the analyzer (0 when not operating).
Pass Energy Pass energy of the analyzer. Left column is the target voltage that you set; right column is the current value of the analyzer (0 when not operating).
Mode and Range Left column is the lens mode; right column is the energy range of the power supply.
Dwell Time

The dwell time for each acquisition point.

 

Click the Activate button to send your settings to the analyzer. The best way of viewing the signal depends on your detector:

■    For 2D detectors, open the Live View and click the icon. The raw 2D data is shown.

■    For SCD/ MCD analyzers, define a spectrum with Fixed Energies mode and use the overwrite mode for data acquisition. This repeats the acquisition of a single energy, allowing you to view the signal as a function of time.

 

 

If there is no signal, you may need to adjust the detector voltage in the Calibration Voltages tab.

 

To stop acquisition:

■    Click the Stop button.

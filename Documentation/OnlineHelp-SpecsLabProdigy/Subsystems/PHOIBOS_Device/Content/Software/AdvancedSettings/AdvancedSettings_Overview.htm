Advanced Settings

On clicking the button at the bottom of the Device Control, the view expands to show a set of tabs that contain further items for controlling the analyzer and detector. The values of the detector (and screen, for 2D detectors) voltage are also shown.

 

The following tabs are available, although some may not be present for your detector:

■    Settings

■    Calib. Voltages

■    Para. Voltages

■    Detector (CCD detectors only)

■    Calibration

■    Std. Channels (MCD detectors only)

 

The contents of some of the tabs will depend on your analyzer and detector.

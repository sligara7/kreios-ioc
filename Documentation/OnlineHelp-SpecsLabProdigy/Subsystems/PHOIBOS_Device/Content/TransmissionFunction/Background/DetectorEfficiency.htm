Detector Efficiency

The detector efficiency depends on the conversion energy of the electrons, Ec. The graph below shows how the detector efficiency varies as a function of conversion energy.

 

In some cases, it is possible to operate the detector in such a way that D is constant. In this case, you do not need to include a factor for the detector efficiency in the transmission function, as any absolute value will be lost as the function is normalized.

 

In other cases, Ec = Ep. For this, you need to find D for your detector. The curve will be similar to that in the figure above.

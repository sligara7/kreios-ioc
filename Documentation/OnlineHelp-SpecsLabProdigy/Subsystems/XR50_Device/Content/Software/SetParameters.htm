Setting Source Parameters

From the initial view of the Device Control, you can clicking the icon to show the X-ray operating parameters. This adds the following features for controlling the source:

■    Power. This is calculated from the emission current and the high voltage. The maximum power is limited by a setting in the power supply—this is dependent on the anode type.

■    High Voltage. The anode voltage, in the range 0…15 kV.

 

The emission current is also shown for information.

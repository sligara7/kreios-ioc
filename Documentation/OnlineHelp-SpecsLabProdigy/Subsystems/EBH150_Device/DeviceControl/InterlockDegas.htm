Interlock Indicators and Degas Feature

When you click the icon once in the initial view, the interlocks and their status are shown. All need to be green for instrument operation. A red indicator means the interlock has tripped.

 

NOTE:
The temperature sensors also have color indicators in this view. Green means the sensor is working correctly; red that there is a fault.

 

This view also shows the Degas button. Clicking this button ramps the filament slowly up to degas or condition the filament. You should perform this after bakeout or after fitting a new filament.

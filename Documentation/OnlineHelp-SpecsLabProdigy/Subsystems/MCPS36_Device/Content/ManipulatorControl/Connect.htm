Connecting to the Motors

Before controlling the motors, you need to establish a connection with the hardware.

 

To connect to the motors:

1. Locate the Manipulator in the Device Controls.
2. Click the Connect button. After making the connection, the current motor settings are shown, as well as the status. This shows the current position of the motors as well as their status ("idle" or "busy"). If a motor is moving, the position is constantly updated.

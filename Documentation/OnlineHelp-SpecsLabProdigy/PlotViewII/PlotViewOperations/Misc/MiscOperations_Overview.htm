Miscellaneous Operations

The miscellaneous operations are a collection of other functions that do not fit in with other operations. Operations available are:

■    Arithmetic mean—calculate the average value of points in a selected region.

■    Dead time correction—compensate for the dead time in detector acquisition.

■    Least squares gradient—calculates the gradient at each point in the spectrum using a first order least squares fit.

■    Linear operations—add offsets and scaling factors to data.

■    Spin Asymmetry for VLEED—calculate the spin component of a signal from a spin-resolved detector.

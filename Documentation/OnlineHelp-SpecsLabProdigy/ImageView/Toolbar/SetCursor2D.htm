Setting Cursor Positions

You can set the position of two cursors in the 2D viewer. These allow you to mark the position of peaks and other features; you can, for example, use them to measure the distance or energy difference between features.

 

When setting cursors, note the following points:

■    If you select another mouse tool (e.g. the Zoom mode), the cursor is sticky and remains in its position until you reactivate the cursor.

■    The and icons toggle the display of the cursors in the image pane.

 

To display a cursor in the image pane:

1. Click the icon or the icon. These activate the yellow and blue cursors respectively.
2. Click a point in the chart. The cursor will be displayed with the center at the selected point. The status bar at the bottom of the 2D viewer will show the coordinates of the cursor center point.

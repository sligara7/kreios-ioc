Fixed Energies

The fixed energy (FE) mode is a simple mode that allows you to monitor a single kinetic energy for a specified time. Particles with a defined kinetic energy pass through the analyzer and are detected. This mode is used when setting up or checking the operation of the analyzer, as well as for investigating the stability of the excitation source.

 

Since the mode measures intensity as a function of time, signals from multiple channels are not of interest. SpecsLab Prodigy therefore treats all channels as a single detector and sums the signal from all channels.

Devices

Many SPECS devices and other third party instruments can be controlled by SpecsLab Prodigy. There are two ways to access devices:

  • As standalone devices. You can access many, or all, features that are available on the front panel of the power supply.
  • As part of an experiment. The device can be added to the schedule and activated with the desired parameter values at certain points in the experiment.

The list on the left only contains devices for which a license is required.

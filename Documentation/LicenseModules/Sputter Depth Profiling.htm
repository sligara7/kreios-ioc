Sputter Depth Profiling

Depth profiling with an ion gun involves removing the surface layer(s) with ions, then measuring the chemical composition of the newly exposed surface. In this way, you can measure the bulk chemical composition of the sample.

The sputter depth profiling module allows automation of depth profiling experiments. In addition to performing a normal scan, it can control a SPECS IQE 12/38 or IQE 11/35 ion gun, allowing user-definable sputter/ measurement cycles.
